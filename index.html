OUR PROGRAM

Our 9-month program is divided into 12 modules. The last module is a 6-week intensive final project where students work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at Eptech (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week, students are supported online in their self-study, homework and peer learning.

Our Core Values

Community

We are largely dependent on volunteers. That's what makes us an engaged grassroots community, as our students call it 'the HYF family'

Diversity & inclusiveness

Our program is adapted to suit people with different backgrounds, often in vulnerable situations. We teach on Sundays to allow everyone to take control of their personal lives during the week.

Commitment

Our program is free of charge, although we ask for strong commitment during our 7 month-journey. And you know what? many alumni stay actively involved!

Open

Sharing is caring. Our curriculum is 100% open. We believe in knowledge for the many, not the few.
